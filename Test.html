This context memo aims to provide an overview of my research proposal on the topic of cancer treatments and immunotherapies. This proposal aims to explore and compare different therapeutic approaches to cancers, specifically comparing three types of T-cell immunotherapies against conventional (radiation and chemotherapy) methods.
